About Us

Since 1985, Shree Maruti has been a leading courier and logistics company in India, and one of the most trustworthy names, when transporting an item from one place to another is concerned. With its headquarters based in Ahmedabad (Gujarat)and corporate office in Mumbai, (Maharashtra), the company is proud to have a legacy of not just an admired brand name, but also, Trust, and Reliability throughout all the years. We have undertaken to set up a Digital Transformation journey in an endeavour to transport faster, safer, and more efficient services to its customers.

Shree Maruti is one of the top 5 leading courier and logistics companies in India with the most extensive network, working expediently 24x7, across 128 operational hubs,4858 pin codes, 3233 channel partners located across the country. Now, with digitalisation, the sky has become the limit and Shree Maruti commits to be the best option in India. We have developed a state-of-the-art IT platform that can help it deliver a great customer experience at every touchpoint.
