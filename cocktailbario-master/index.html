    Our main concoction

    $COC is the native ERC-20 token of the CocktailBar ecosystem, operating on the Ethereum Blockchain. It serves as the cornerstone of our platform, facilitating transactions, governance, and incentive mechanisms within our community.

    • Total Supply: 50,000 tokens
    • Integral Role: Holders participate in profit sharing and decision-making processes.
    • Dynamic Rewards: Benefit from a range of rewards and opportunities as the platform evolves.
    • No Buy/Sell Tax
    • Liquidity Locked

    Our profit-sharing model ensures that the success of the ecosystem is directly reflected in the value of $COC, providing a dynamic and rewarding experience for all stakeholders. In summary, $COC is not just a token but a representation of participation and investment in the CocktailBar ecosystem.

    As we embark on new projects and initiatives, we look forward to growing alongside our community of $COC holders, ensuring a prosperous and engaging future for all.
